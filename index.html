Terraform Ceramic Vase

$120.00

Hand-thrown in our coastal studio, the Terraform Ceramic Vase features a unique textural finish inspired by weathered cliffs. Its organic silhouette serves as a sculptural statement piece, even when empty.

Each piece is individually crafted, ensuring that no two vases are exactly alike. Finished in a matte bone glaze.

Dimensions ↓

H 24cm x W 18cm. Mouth opening 6cm.

Care Guide ↓

Hand wash only with mild detergent. Not microwave safe.

The Philosophy of page

At page, we believe that the objects we surround ourselves with define the quality of our daily existence. Our curation process is rigorous and poetic, focusing on craftsmanship that honors the raw integrity of materials. Every item in our collection is a testament to the beauty of the human touch, designed to bridge the gap between functional utility and fine art.

Sustainability is not a feature of our brand; it is the foundation. We prioritize longevity over trends, advocating for a "buy once, keep forever" mentality. By sourcing responsibly and partnering with independent artisans who share our commitment to the earth, we ensure that every page object contributes to a future of thoughtful consumption and reduced waste.

We are a part of the "quiet home" movement—a return to intentional living where space is given room to breathe. In a world of constant digital noise, page provides a tactile anchor. We invite you to slow down, appreciate the grain of the wood, the cool temperature of stone, and the gentle silhouette of a well-placed vessel. This is your life, curated.
